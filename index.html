Music Panes (Perfect Fifth Detection)

• Drag a pane to move it.
• Drag its lower‑right corner to resize it.
• Double‑click on empty space to add a new pane.
• Double‑click on a pane to toggle its slider.
• Right‑click on a pane to delete it.

Each pane is assigned a random dark-to-light mapping preset (e.g. blue→yellow, red→yellow, purple→orange, or green→yellow).
The slider (with a range of 0–800 and step 0.1) controls both the lightness (L) and hue (H) so that frequency changes are very fine‑grained (≤1 Hz per step).
A white line is drawn between two panes whose frequencies are a perfect fifth apart (ratio ≈1.5).

(Click anywhere to start audio.)
